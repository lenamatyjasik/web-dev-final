About us

Why swim when you could dive?! And what’s more...you could dive with shoals of fish on a WW2 submarine....we all have!

Under Sea Adventurers is one of Norway's oldest scuba diving clubs, in existence since 1981. Our dive program consists of local dives, trips to the Lofoten and exotic overseas dive locations. Our monthly meetings feature informative and educational speakers.

Our mission is to become the 'go to' community for anyone wanting to learn about, enjoy and protect the underwater world.
